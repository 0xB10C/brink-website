Brink was founded in 2020 with generous funding from John Pfeffer and Wences Casares. We are 100% funded by donations from members of the public and sponsorship from Bitcoin businesses.

If we are successful in our application, donations to Brink from U.S. taxpayers will be tax-exempt, including those made during our application period.
